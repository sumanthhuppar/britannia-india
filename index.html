ABOUT US

Established in Kolkata in 1892, Britannia is a household name in India, and one of the country's leading food products companies. Over the years, Britannia has grown to become India's most loved and adopted snacking companies and our operations span across the country to the world.

THE WADIA GROUP

Wadia Group is one of the oldest conglomerates in India with diversified interests spanning textiles, chemicals, real estate, aviation, consumer products, textiles, chemicals and food processing. Four companies in the Wadia Group, which form part of our Group companies, are listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange.
